# CatSDK - Gemini 3.5 & Gemini 3.1 Pro



Gemini 3.5 delivers significantly improved reasoning, coding, and multimodal capabilities over its predecessors, making it Google's flagship model for complex agentic workflows and long-context understanding. 



Gemini 3.1 Pro remains a highly efficient and cost-effective option, offering excellent performance for most production use cases while maintaining strong speed and lower latency. 



Together they form a strong dual-model strategy: use Gemini 3.5 for maximum intelligence and Gemini 3.1 Pro for scale and efficiency.
